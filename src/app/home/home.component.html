For many stations / devices in Fablab it usually requires intensive instruction or instruction. The FabInfo tutorial system provides a remedy by clearly displaying and explaining the correct handling of each of the FabLab devices.

Frequently recurring questions such as according to the settings, the materials to be used, etc., you can also find them here, in the corresponding category. Thus, we want to minimize or prevent the risk for you as a user, as well as for the devices as possible.
